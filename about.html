About

I like working in a very capricious way. I like being strongly influenced by where I am and how I feel.
I make different kinds of work: sometimes my work is very airy, soft and quiet, and sometimes I make very playful and colourful things Sometimes I am able to bring those two parts of myself together in one image. 

My sculptures and drawings say something about being alone. When I make these works I feel like a kid that is playing alone, but is totally fine with that. My work arose from a kind of stream of consciousness.
